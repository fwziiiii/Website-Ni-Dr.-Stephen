The Coral Council

Meet The Coral Council — a trio of curious minds embarking on their journey as first-year students in the Bachelor of Science in Marine Biology program at Mindanao State University. United by our shared fascination with the ocean, we're setting sail on an academic adventure full of discovery, salty surprises, and the occasional lab mishap.

As freshmen navigating our first year, we’re learning the ropes — from lectures on ecosystems to hands-on fieldwork by the shore. While we may be at the start of our academic voyage, our enthusiasm for marine science runs deep, and we’re diving in with wide eyes and open minds.

Together, we’re more than just classmates — we’re a growing crew bound by teamwork, curiosity, and the dream of making waves in marine biology. Whether we’re studying coral polyps or cracking up over sea-themed memes, The Coral Council is here to learn, explore, and inspire others to join us beneath the surface.

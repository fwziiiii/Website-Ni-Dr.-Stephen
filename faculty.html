The Coral Council

We’re thrilled to introduce the brilliant minds steering the ship of our marine biology program! These esteemed faculty members are more than just educators — they’re passionate explorers of the ocean’s mysteries, guiding students through the fascinating currents of marine science.

With deep expertise, innovative teaching approaches, and a shared love for the sea, they’ve helped chart the course of our institution’s success. From coral reefs to classroom reefs, their commitment to academic excellence and student discovery has left a lasting impact on every wave of learners that passes through our doors.

Whether decoding dolphin behavior or debugging lab equipment, their contributions continue to shape our community and inspire the next generation of ocean stewards. Anchored in experience and driven by curiosity, they remain an essential part of our ever-evolving marine journey.
